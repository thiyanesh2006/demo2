What is Customer Journey Mapping?

Definition

Customer Journey Mapping (CJM) is a strategic tool that visually represents a customer’s experience as they interact with a business or service. It outlines the complete journey from the customer's perspective, starting from their initial contact and spanning all touchpoints to the desired outcome. The map helps organizations understand the thoughts, emotions, and behaviors of customers throughout their journey.

Purpose:

The primary purpose of Customer Journey Mapping is to improve the overall customer experience by identifying and addressing pain points, enhancing positive interactions, and aligning internal processes with customer needs.
